About Lazyreader

Lazyreader is a reading tool designed to make digital reading more efficient and easy on the eyes. When we read traditionally, the rate at which our eyes scan the page must be closely held at the rate we wish to be reading and can be exceeded by the rate at which we comprehend the words, thus becoming the limiting factor in how quickly we can read the text. Other digital speedreading tools have attempted to eliminate this element of inconsistency by, at a given moment, displaying only those words that would be at the center of a consistently-paced reader's focus. Lazyreader improves on this idea by continuing to display those words in focus at the center of the screen, while also displaying several lines of context above and below them to accomodate variations in comprehension speed and to maintain the use of peripheral vision we have access to in traditional reading.

Instructions:
1. Paste text in raw form into the entry field
2. Set WPM (words per minute) at a comfortable rate
3. Select 'Start Reading'
(Optional) While reading, hold SPACEBAR to temporarily halve reading speed
